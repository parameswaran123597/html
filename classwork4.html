Welcome To The Cricket Scribe Podcast

Welcome to the The Cricket Scribe Podcast, the weekly show that digs deeper into the sport you love. Tired of surface-level commentary? We're here to offer in-depth tactical analysis, historical deep-dives, and exclusive player stories you won't hear anywhere else. From the nail-biting finishes of the Ashes to the high-octane drama of the IPL, our hosts—former first-class cricketer Mark Ellis and sports journalist Anya Sharma—bring their unique blend of expertise and passion to every episode. Whether you’re a long-time fan or a curious newcomer, join our conversation as we explore the good, the bad, and the brilliant of the gentleman's game.
