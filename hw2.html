Mahabharata--Veda Vyasa--The Mahabharata is a vast ancient Indian Sanskrit epic about the war between two sets of cousins, the Pandavas and the Kauravas, for control of the throne of Hastinapura.

Ramayana--Valmiki--The Ramayana is an ancient Indian epic attributed to the sage Valmiki, which tells the story of Rama, an incarnation of the god Vishnu. It follows his journey from a prince of Ayodhya to his exile, the abduction of his wife Sita by the demon king Ravana, and the eventual battle to rescue her.

Wings of fire--Dr.APJ Abdul Kalam--Wings of Fire is the autobiography of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, chronicling his journey from a young boy in Rameswaram to becoming a renowned scientist and the President of India.
